Banksouksawda.com exhibition will happen at LT Gallery between October 17 & October 24.

For a decade, the Central Bank of Lebanon has been creating promotional material out of shredded local currency. Today, we have created a re-interpretation of the renowned Lebanese Lira bill out of this material, for no other reason than that we can. 

This exhibition will also showcase the oldest cheques issued by Imperial Ottoman Bank in the 1800's, with editions reaching up to 1990's. At this exhibition, we will reveal our mission.

This banksouksawda.com currency is 99% handmade.com
